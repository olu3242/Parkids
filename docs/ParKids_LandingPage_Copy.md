## 13. FAQ SECTION

### Q: Is Par-Kids safe for my child's data?
> Absolutely. Par-Kids is built with family privacy as a non-negotiable. We are COPPA-compliant, never sell data, and your child's check-in responses are never used for advertising. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

### Q: What age range is Par-Kids for?
> Par-Kids is designed for children ages 6 to 17, with age-appropriate question sets for three groups: Early (6–10), Preteen (11–13), and Teen (14–17). The experience adapts automatically.

### Q: Does my child have their own login?
> Yes. Your child has their own child-mode access, managed through your parent account. You control what they can see and do. Children cannot access the parent dashboard.

### Q: What if we miss a check-in?
> No guilt here. Par-Kids will prompt you to reschedule a missed check-in. Your streak continues — what matters is consistency over perfection.

### Q: Can both parents access the same child?
> Yes. Par-Kids supports co-parent access with configurable visibility settings. Both parents can stay informed without conflict.

### Q: How long does a check-in take?
> Most check-ins take 15–25 minutes when done together. You can also split it — child completes their part first, then the parent follows up.

### Q: Is this a therapy or counseling app?
> No. Par-Kids is a family connection and growth tool, not a mental health service. However, monthly summary reports can be shared with therapists or counselors if you choose.
